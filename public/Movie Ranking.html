My top 3 movies of all-time

Slum Dog Millionaire

Slumdog Millionaire is a 2008 British drama film directed by Danny Boyle, written by Simon Beaufoy, and co-directed by Loveleen Tandan. It is based on the novel Q & A (2005) by Indian author Vikas Swarup. The film stars Dev Patel as Jamal Malik, a Mumbai teen who becomes a contestant on the Hindi version of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? and reflects on his life in the slums of Mumbai.

Philadelphia

Philadelphia is a 1993 American legal drama film directed by Jonathan Demme, written by Ron Nyswaner, and starring Tom Hanks and Denzel Washington. The film tells the story of Andrew Beckett

The Great Debate

The Great Debaters is a 2007 American biographical drama film directed by Denzel Washington, based on the true story of the debate team from Wiley College, a historically black college in Texas, in the 1930s. The film stars Denzel Washington, Forest Whitaker, and Jurnee Smollett.
